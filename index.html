Katherine Geist

Genomics of social conflict & cooperation

I am in the final year of my PhD in the laboratory of Joan Strassmann and David Queller at Washington University in St. Louis. My major thrust as a graduate student has been to ask whether the genomic signature of conflict arising during cooperative actions looks like what we see during other types of conflict, e.g., host-pathogen conflict.
